About the Workshop

The workshop's primary objective is to create a platform for connecting various communities, fostering collaboration for the advancement of a compositional theory of decision making.

This workshop is open to participants from diverse backgrounds and experience levels, catering to both newcomers and seasoned professionals.

Attendees can expect to gain valuable insights into addressing intricate decision-making problems, as the workshop will feature success stories and practical tools from applied category theory.

Distinguished experts in the field will deliver talks, showcasing the practical implementation of these tools to tackle real-world issues. These presentations will also offer valuable perspectives on potential directions for future research endeavors, and for creating a new, interdisciplinary community around these topics.
